#简体 version
抵达附近时已经挤的水泄不通，到处都是一大片闹烘烘。
晓瑜把我抓的死紧，整个人都贴上手臂。
-By the time we arrive, the street is jam-packed with people everywhere.
-Xiao Yu holds me very tightly, as if she is attached to my shoulder. 

李肇维：
好。。。。。。好挤！怎么会这么挤！基隆的人有这么多！？
-It’s… It’s so crowded! How could it be crowded like this! Does Keelung have this many people!?

张晓瑜：
肇维。。。。。。我。。。。。。我快不行了。。。。。。
-Zhao Wei… I… I can’t go on…

李肇维：
等一下！撑住啊！不要晕倒在这里！
-Wait! Hold yourself together! Don’t faint here!

张晓瑜：
对不起。。。。。。肇维。。。。。。到了这种时候，我还是这么讨人厌。。。。。。可是我真的很高兴能够再次遇见你。。。。。。
-I’m sorry… Zhao Wei… At times like this, I’m so troublesome… But I’m really happy that I could meet you again…

李肇维：
不要讲这种自己好像快要死掉的台词啦!
-Don’t speak as if you’re about to die!

李肇维：
来了！
-It’s coming!

一股哗然的骚动从港口一波波卷到路口。松动了拥挤的人群，也让我的双脚重新落地。
-A burst of commotion ripples from the harbour to the road. It breaks up the crowd and my feet touch the ground once again.

李元德：
啊？哥？
-Ah? Big brother?

熟悉的语气勾起了我的注意。
-My attention is caught by a familiar voice.

此时，从旁边勉强穿过人潮凑了过来的，正是我的弟弟，李元德。
-In the same moment, someone forces their way through the crowd to my side, and it turns out to be my little brother, Li Yuan De.

李肇维：
元德？
-Yuan De?

李元德：
你也来啦，小只鱼。
-You also came along, Little Fish.
(Note: Xiao Yu’s name sounds like “little fish” in Chinese)

张晓瑜：
小只鱼不是给你叫的。。。。。。
-Don’t call me Little Fish…

李肇维：
你们两个都这么大了，就别跟小时候一样斗嘴了吧。
-You two have grown up, so stop bickering like when you were little.

李元德：
我还以为哥不关心台湾的事情。
-Big brother, I thought you didn’t care about Taiwan’s affairs.

李肇维：
是啊，真说起来，我比较关心你。
-Yes, honestly speaking, I care more about you.

李元德：
不用担心，在国民政府的统治下，我们台湾人的日子一定会更好。
-Don’t worry. Under the governance of the National Government, we Taiwanese will have even better days.

元德手边拿着一根大旗。
是我今天看到的，最大面的国民政府旗。
-Yuan De is holding a large flag by his side.
-It’s the largest National Government flag I have seen today.

李元德：
革命万岁！民主万岁！祖国万岁！台湾人的日子来临了！我们终于变回中国人了！
-Long live the revolution! Long live democracy! Long live the homeland! The days for Taiwanese have arrived! We have finally returned to being Chinese!

元德的呐喊触动了所有人的鼓噪。
鼓噪中，某个很大很大的东西与港口的人群产生了碰撞。
-Yuan De’s loud shouting stirs the crowd into an uproar.
-Amongst the uproar, something very, very big collides with the people at the harbour.

一波超大的浪潮把我们三个一口气打散！
-A huge wave of people knocks the three of us apart!

张晓瑜：
肇。。。。。。肇维！
-Zhao… Zhao Wei!

李肇维：
晓。。。。。。晓瑜！
-Xiao… Xiao Yu!

我们彼此伸出手，却没能碰到。
-We stretch out our hands, but are unable to reach each other.

元德的大旗激烈摇晃着。
他没有被人潮冲走，反而逆流向前挺进。
-Yuan De’s large flag is vigorously waving back and forth.
-Instead of being swept away by the wave of people, Yuan De moves forward against it.

我被人潮一路推着前进，直到完全离开才停。
-I’m pushed along by the wave of people until I’ve completely left the area.

#End scene
